Dragunov

Dragunov is a Russian agent that first appeared in Tekken 5. His gameplay revolves around his Running 2, a plus on block launcher, and Down 2, a quick low to chip away at an opponent's health. His long limbs and good frame data make him a very good rushdown and poke heavy character, but he has many tools that can be used in any style of play.

Top Moves

  1. Running 2
  2. Down 2
  3. Back 1+2

Movement Options

  • Above average forward dash
  • Average backdash
  • Above average sidestep
  • Average run speed
