Basics

Tekken 7 is a 3D fighting game made by Bandai Namco. This game is based off of four button system linked to each of the character's limbs. There are three types of attacks, High Mid and Low options hit different types of defensive positions. There are also many different types of movement options to try and maintain your character's optimal range

Button Mapping

  1. left punch
  2. right punch
  3. left kick
  4. right kick

Movement Options

  • Forward walk/dash
  • Backward walk/dash
  • Sidesteps to either side
  • Running forward

Guard Options

Guard Type High Mid Low
Standing blocked blocked hit
Crouching miss hit blocked
